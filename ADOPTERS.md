# GALOSH adopters

Organizations and projects that use GALOSH — in products, services, internal
pipelines, or research — **or that drew on GALOSH as a reference or
inspiration** for their own implementation or design. Being listed here is
**voluntary and with the adopter's consent**; Apache-2.0 imposes no obligation
to report usage. Reference-only use counts just as much: if GALOSH informed
your work in any way, we'd be glad to hear from you.

## How to get listed

Any one of:
- Open a pull request adding a row to the table below,
- Open an issue at <https://github.com/luxgrain/GALOSH/issues> titled
  `adopter: <name>`, or
- Reach out privately if you prefer not to be listed publicly — feedback from
  unlisted users is just as welcome.

Suggested row format: organization/project, how GALOSH is used — direct use
or "reference" — (one line), and an optional link.

| Adopter | Usage (direct / reference) | Link |
|---|---|---|
| *(your organization here)* | | |

## Research use

If GALOSH contributed to a publication, please cite the paper (see
`CITATION.cff` or the README) — citations are the easiest way to tell us the
work mattered.
